    Welcome

    Sandbox is an Online Treasure Hunt Organized as a part of REVOZO'14 .A National Level Technical Symposium Organized by Department of Information Technology Kumaraguru College of Technology Coimbatore

    Function

    • A user can signup and login to Get Started
    • they will be presented with 4 blended images which will have a connection between them
    • If they answer correctly they will be redirected to next question
    • In the meanwhile they can check their progress against others competitors in LEADER BOARD.
    • In Forum they can discuss and ask clues
